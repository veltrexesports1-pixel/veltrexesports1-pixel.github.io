Building Competitive Esports And Gaming Culture In Morocco

VeltRex Esports is a Moroccan esports organization built around competition, structure, and long term growth. VeltRex combines pro teams, community building, and strong execution to create one of the most ambitious gaming brands in Morocco.

Who We Are

A Modern Esports Brand

VeltRex is more than a team. It is a structured gaming brand built to connect competitive performance, community energy, and brand opportunities under one strong identity.

With teams across multiple titles and a growing presence in the Moroccan gaming scene, VeltRex is positioned as both a competitive force and a media-driven esports brand.
